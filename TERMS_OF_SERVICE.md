# Terms of Service — Role Restore Bot

**Last updated:** June 19, 2026

## Description

Role Restore Bot automatically saves the roles of members who leave a Discord server and restores those roles when they rejoin.

## Usage

By adding this bot to your server, you agree to the following:

1. The bot will store role data for members who leave servers where it is active.
2. The bot will automatically reassign stored roles when members rejoin.
3. Banned members will **not** have their roles saved or restored.
4. Members can delete their own stored data at any time using the `/deleteme` command.

## Limitations

- The bot can only restore roles that are below its own role in the server hierarchy.
- Deleted roles cannot be restored.
- The bot requires the **Manage Roles** and **Server Members Intent** permissions to function.
